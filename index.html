Putting off claiming

You don’t have to start claiming when you reach State Pension age and you no longer have to pay National Insurance."

If you put off claiming, you may get extra State Pension when you do claim it. The extra amount is paid with your State Pension and may be taxable.

You’ll need to put off claiming for at least 9 weeks and your State Pension will increase by 1% for every 9 weeks you put off claiming. This works out at just under 5.8% for every full year you put off claiming.
